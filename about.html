What is Moodji?
Based on the Argus Labs Mobile SDK for ambient sensing, the Gracenote Rythm API & the Deezer API, we're building Moodji. Moodji senses in real-time how you're feeling, and creates an adaptive playlist filled with music you like that matches your current mood.
